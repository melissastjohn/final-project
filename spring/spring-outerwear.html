Spring Outerwear

With spring comes unpredictable weather — April showers, brisk mornings and chillier nights. For those transitional days leading to summer, Spring 2013 outerwear offers a multitude of options with new takes on seasonal classics. The label’s pullover anoraks — feature lightweight cotton-poly blends perfect for warm-weather layering, while details like front pouch pockets and adjustable hoods and hems make for timeless, versatile designs. Hood-less — but equally sharp, a duo of riffs on a jacket. The former boasts two-tone brushed twill and fleece construction while the latter channels spring with floral canvas and tonal quill embroidery.

Spring Casual

Get inspired this season for fashionable casual wear! Looking at some of the amazing dresses, cool shoes, and fabulous colors combined, which were based off of the New York Fashion Frends of 2013!

A big one for a casual afternoon is mixing prints! Because army is big this year, a great army blouse with a printed or patterned skirt could really be eye catching! Another big mix of colors for the season is red, black, and leopard. Pairing a red blouse, black jeans, and some cheetah shoes is a perfect and comfortable outfit for everyday!

Love your comfortable house tees? You can dress them up and still look chic on a day out for lunch. Just pair them up with a blazer and a nice pair of jeans and your set!

Another big one is bright colors silks. They are soft, comfortable, and flowy, perfect for the season! Pairing a purple tank top with a long silk high-waisted skirt is an amazing casual but stil classy outfit.

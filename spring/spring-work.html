Spring Work


We all want to look fashionable 24 hours a day, but adding a dose of style between 9 and 5 can be a challenge. Try our work-appropriate tips to incorporate spring's trends into your office looks (without getting a call from HR.)

One great lookis a well-fitting sheath, a wardrobe essential. The key two elements to an outfit like this is the wide belt, which gives an hourglass shape. Also the vertical print gives a very slimming effect.

If you feel like waring a blazer add it with a nice cropped trouser cut and emblazoned T-shirt. It gives the traditional suit a whole new spin.

If your going for a more conservative look, wear a mid-calf length skirt, added with a slim blouse or blazer. It really balances out the volume down below!

Winter Outerwear

Being able to leave the house comfortable and warm is a must for the Winter season. However, looking good in what you are wearing is also very important. A big thing that has come back this year is prints. Whether it be leopard or army style, they are both perfect for the season. Wearing a faux-fur leopard or cheetah patterns puffy jacket is always stylish and comfortable. However, if the weather is not too chilly, a army styled light jacket or vest is a very cute look for the day.

Pairing these two types of jacket styles up with knitted scarves, hats, and gloves are also an essential. The knitted hats are back and wearing a neutral or bright color with either of these jacket ideas is perfect!

Winter Night Out

Wearing a black slimmy dressed added with a faux-fur jacket can really make you stand out for any evening! It is very easy to pull together, but also comfortable and chic as well. Also pairing a light pink or beige up to the knee dress with a pair of tights, knitted hat, and a pair of boots will also have you looking fashionable and comfortable as well.

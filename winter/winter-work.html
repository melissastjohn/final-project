Winter Work

Being able to look good for work in such a cold season can at times be stressful. All anyone wants to do is put on some sweatpants and call it a day! However, there are outfits that can still make a day at the office comfortable. One great outfit is wearing a long sleeved cashmere or chunky sweater tucked into a velvet high waisted neutral colored skirt. Afraid of it being too cold? Adding a pair of thick tights, such as white ones and a comfrtable boot can really pull the whole outfit together.Another great otufit idea is adding leather. Leather is very big for 2013 and will continue to be. Adding leather leaggings, a leather jccket, and a plain tee underneath can keep it simple but still fashionable.
